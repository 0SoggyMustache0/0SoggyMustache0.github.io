WMATM Vehicles





Description

Minecraft highly customizable vehicle mod

Everyone needs to move around their world quickly, but horses and minecarts don't do the job or look cool, that's why we have made WMATM Vehicles. This mod adds in Cars, Planes, Bikes, Motorcycles and more, BUT they are all super customization there are over 1,000 customization combinations for some vehicles!, Besides customization we have worked very hard to make the vehicles realistic, if you drive off a cliff the engine will break and the car will eventually explode, we have also made the vehicles less prone to damage so if someone tries to kill your car they can't attack it.
